What Not to Eat

List of Foods to Avoid for a Healthy Lifestyle

Processed Foods:

  • Fast food
  • Frozen meals
  • Processed meats (sausages, bacon, deli meats)
  • Chips and snacks
  • Sugary breakfast cereals
  • Sugary drinks (sodas, energy drinks, sweetened beverages)
  • Packaged desserts and sweets

Added Sugars:

  • Sugary snacks and candies
  • Sugary drinks (sodas, fruit juices, sports drinks)
  • Sweetened breakfast cereals
  • Flavored yogurts
  • Processed foods with high sugar content

Trans Fats:

  • Fried foods
  • Processed snacks (cakes, cookies, pastries)
  • Margarine and shortening
  • Processed spreads and dips
  • Some packaged baked goods

Sodium-rich Foods:

  • Salty snacks (chips, pretzels)
  • Canned soups and broths
  • Processed meats (bacon, sausages)
  • Packaged sauces and dressings
  • Fast food

Artificial Sweeteners:

  • Diet sodas and drinks
  • Sugar-free candies and desserts
  • Some low-calorie or sugar-free packaged foods
